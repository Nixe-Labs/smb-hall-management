1. About This Guide

SMB Hall Management is the web application used to run the day-to-day business of the hall — from taking a booking, recording advance payments and bills, tracking expenses, to generating customer invoices and financial reports.

This guide walks through every screen and function in the order you are most likely to use them.

2. Getting Started

What you need

  • A modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, or Firefox).
  • A user account created for you by the administrator.
  • An internet connection.

Opening the app

Open the app in your browser:

https://nixe-labs.github.io/smb-hall-management/

You will land on the Sign In page. Bookmark this link for quick access.

3. User Roles & What You Can Do

The app uses three roles. Your role determines which buttons and pages you see.

Role View Create / Edit Delete Admin Settings
Admin Yes Yes Yes Yes
Staff Yes Yes No No
Viewer Yes No No No

If a button (for example, New Booking or Delete) is not visible to you, it means your role does not allow that action. Ask an admin if you need elevated access.

4. Signing In

  1. On the Sign In screen, enter your email and password.
  2. Click Sign In.
  3. On success, you are taken to the Dashboard. On failure, a red toast appears at the top-right describing the problem.
Tip: Click the small eye icon at the end of the password field to show or hide your password. If you forget your password, contact an admin — they can reset it from User Management.

5. The Dashboard

The Dashboard is your home screen — an at-a-glance summary of the hall's performance.

KPI cards (top row)

  • Total Revenue — sum of all bill items from past (completed) bookings that were not cancelled.
  • Total Expenses — sum of all expenses ever recorded.
  • Net Profit — Revenue minus Expenses. Green when positive, red when negative.
  • Total Bookings — count of all non-cancelled bookings, with chips for Upcoming vs Done.

Revenue Analytics chart

A bar chart of monthly revenue for the selected year. Use the left / right arrow buttons next to the year to move between years. You cannot go into the future — the right arrow is disabled for years after the current one.

This Week panel

  • Lists every non-cancelled event in the current calendar week (Sunday to Saturday).
  • Each card shows the customer name, date (Today, Tomorrow, or weekday + date), and a status badge.
  • Click any event to jump straight to its booking detail.
  • Click View Calendar to open the calendar page.

6. Managing Bookings

Open the Bookings page from the side navigation — this is the master list of every booking in the system.

Reading the list

ColumnMeaning
DateThe function date.
CustomerThe customer name.
RentHall rent for that booking (hidden on small screens).
StatusAuto-calculated: Upcoming (future), Today, Completed (past), or Cancelled.

Click anywhere on a row to open its details.

Searching and filtering

  • Search customer — type a name, press Enter or the search icon.
  • From date / To date — narrow to a date range. Selecting a date applies the filter immediately.
  • On mobile, tap Filters to open these controls. A green badge shows how many filters are active.
  • Click Clear to reset all filters.

Sorting and paging

  • Click any column header (Date, Customer, Rent, Status) to sort.
  • Use the pagination bar at the bottom to change rows-per-page (10, 25, 50) and jump between pages.

Creating a new booking

  1. Click New Booking (top-right). Admins and staff only.
  2. Fill in the form — required fields are marked with *:
    • Function Date *
    • Customer Name *
    • Phone (optional)
    • Address (optional)
    • Rent Amount * — in Indian Rupees.
    • Notes (menu, special arrangements, etc.)
  3. Click Create Booking.
Double-booking guard. If another non-cancelled booking already exists for the selected date, you see a Date Unavailable warning and the booking is not saved. Pick another date or cancel the existing booking first.

7. Booking Calendar

  • Days with confirmed (non-cancelled) bookings are highlighted.
  • Click a date to see the bookings for that day listed below the calendar.
  • Admins and staff can jump to a booking or create a new one directly from here.

8. Inside a Booking

Clicking a booking opens the Booking Details page — the heart of day-to-day operations.

Header

Shows customer name, function date, phone, rent, and the current status tag. On the right, depending on your role, you'll see:

  • Invoice — generate a PDF invoice (see section 9).
  • Cancel — cancel this booking (admin / staff, when not already cancelled).
  • Delete — permanently remove the booking (admin only).

Financial Summary

Auto-calculated panel showing total advance received, total bill amount, total expenses, total deposits, and balance. It updates live as you add or edit entries in any tab.

Tabs

Advances

  • Up to three advances per booking.
  • For each: amount, payment mode (cash / cheque / online), reference or cheque number, bank account, date.

Bill Items

  • Line items that make up the final customer bill.
  • Pick a category (GST Bill, Cleaning, EB, Water, Gas, AC, Room Rent, Generator, etc.).
  • Enter amount and optional note. Totals form the Revenue for the booking.

Expenses

  • Costs the hall incurs that are not passed to the customer.
  • Pick an expense category (Ladies Cleaning, Toilet Cleaning, Staff Payments, Tractor, etc.).
  • Enter amount, date, and any note.

Deposits

  • Money from the customer that you have deposited into a bank account.
  • Pick bank account, amount, date, reference.
  • Helps reconcile which money from this booking has reached the bank.

9. Generating Invoices (PDF)

On the Booking Details page, click Invoice in the header.

  • The system builds a PDF with customer details, rent, all advance payments, every bill line item, and totals.
  • It downloads with a filename like Invoice-<CustomerName>-<Date>.pdf.
  • Open it in any PDF viewer to print or share via email / WhatsApp.

10. Cancelling & Deleting Bookings

Cancel AdminStaff

  • Click Cancel. A confirmation dialog appears.
  • Type CANCEL to confirm.
  • Status becomes Cancelled; the date is free again.
  • Cancelled bookings stay in the system — they no longer count towards revenue, expenses, or totals.

Delete Admin

  • Click Delete. A confirmation dialog appears.
  • This is permanent — all related advances, bill items, expenses, and deposits are deleted.
  • Prefer Cancel unless you specifically want to remove a test or duplicate entry.

11. Reports

  1. Open Reports from the sidebar.
  2. Optionally pick a date range. Leave blank to include all bookings.
  3. The table lists each booking with rent, bill total, expenses, advances.
  4. A totals row shows combined revenue, expenses, and advances for the selected range.

Use this for monthly, quarterly, or yearly reviews.

12. Admin Settings Admin only

Settings is where you configure the lists that every other screen depends on.

Bill Categories

  • The pick-list shown when staff add bill items.
  • Add new (e.g. "Decoration", "Sound System"), rename, or deactivate. Deactivating hides them from future bills but keeps historical records intact.

Expense Categories

Same as Bill Categories, but for expenses.

Bank Accounts

  • The bank accounts used in advances and deposits (e.g. SMB AC, Niranjana AC, Petty Cash).
  • Add new accounts with a name and short code; deactivate unused ones.

User Management

  • Email, role, and creation date for every user.
  • Change a user's role from the dropdown.
  • Only admins can reach this page.
Rule of thumb. Give Viewer to anyone who just needs to look at numbers. Give Staff to front-desk and operations. Keep Admin restricted to owners / senior management.

13. Tips & Troubleshooting

I clicked something and nothing happened.

Look at the top-right corner — the app shows a toast message for every action (green = success, orange = warning, red = error).

"Date Unavailable" when creating a booking.

Another booking exists for that date and has not been cancelled. Filter the Bookings list by that date, then cancel it or pick another date.

I can't see a button someone else can see.

Almost always a role issue. Ask an admin to check your role in User Management.

The dashboard numbers look wrong.

  • Revenue counts only past bookings (function date before today) that are not cancelled.
  • Total Bookings excludes cancelled ones.
  • If you just added a bill item or expense, refresh the page — the dashboard loads once when you open it.

A booking won't delete.

Only admins can delete. Staff should use Cancel instead.

Printing a PDF invoice.

Click Invoice, open the downloaded PDF, and print from your PDF viewer.

Logging out.

Use the logout option in the top-right user menu.
